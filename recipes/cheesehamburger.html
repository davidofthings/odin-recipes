Cheddar Cheese Hamburger

Description:

A simple hamburger recipe if you're hungry and wanting something quick!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • seasoning
  • sliced cheddar cheese
  • lettuce
  • sliced tomatoes
  • hamburger buns

Instructions:

  1. Mix ground beef with preferred seasonings.
  2. Seperate the mixed beef into quarters.
  3. Roll them into a ball in your hand then dip a finger into the middle of the ball.
  4. Cook on the stovetop or grill for better char.
  5. Flip once then serve onto a bun with the cheese, tomato and lettuce.
