Mushroom Omelette

Description:

This is a must try mushroom omelette recipe that will make you wish you had known this recipe sooner!

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 button mushrooms
  • 2 grape tomatoes (optional)
  • grated cheddar cheese
  • half an onion

Instructions:

  1. Dice button mushrooms and onions.
  2. Heat up sauce pan to medium high heat with a little oil.
  3. Saute mushrooms and diced onions.
  4. Beat eggs into bowl with some water and salt and pepper.
  5. Place beaten eggs into sauce pan and let it cook until the top starts to cook.
  6. After a minute fold over the egg omelette and place sliced tomatoes for garnish.
