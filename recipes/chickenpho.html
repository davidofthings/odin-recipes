Chicken Pho

Description:

This is a pho recipe that has been passed down generations with a slight cheat!

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 whole sweet onions
  • pho spice packet (the cheat)
  • thin flat noodles or pho noodles purchased at your local asian market
  • 2 Tbsp fish sauce

Instructions:

  1. Boil large pot of water then throw in whole chicken.
  2. After it starts boiling with the chicken skim the scum off the top.
  3. Put in packet of spices along with the fish sauce.
  4. Boil for 2 hours.
  5. Remove and debone chicken once cooled.
  6. Boil noodles in seperate pot. It should be a quick process.
  7. Place ingredients into a bowl. Garnish with whatever you like. And you're done!
